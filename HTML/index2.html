Welcome to Application Organizer!

We help people who are applying for internships or jobs track their applications and progress. Staying organized during the application process is essential.
This website can be used as a structuring tool to keep track of your progress.

Completed Applications

Keep track of your completed applications by going here. You can add a new application by simply going to the "add a new application." You can also move the application to offers or rejections by clicking on the respected option. By doing so, you will be able to keep your applications in an organized manner.

Saved Applications

Keep track of your Saved Applications by going here. This will help you organize your saved Applications by giving them a priority.

Upcoming Interviews

Keep track of your Upcoming Interviews by going here. This is a great initiative way to stay organized and clear with your schedule. You can add an interview or delete an interview when it is done.

Offers

Got an offer? Keep track of your offers by going here. You can add new offers or delete them too.
